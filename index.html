Hi! I'm Michelle

About Me

Michelle was born in Paris, France. She moved around in childhood between Europe, India, Dubai & Australia, and enjoys traveling to this day.
When she is not teaching students or treating patients, Michelle likes to spend time with her husband, 21 year old son, and 16 year old daughter. She also loves yoga, weight training, and Pilates, which she incorporates into her patients' treatments.

My Credentials

Michelle earned her entry level physiotherapy degree in 1995 from the University of Queensland, Australia. Upon graduating, Michelle moved to London where she worked in the NHS (national health service) up until 1999. In 2000 Michelle returned to University of Queensland to earn her post graduate degree in Physiotherapy specializing in Manual musculoskeletal medicine. Upon completion of her post graduate degree she returned to London to private practice in outpatient orthopedics. In 2001 Michelle moved to Austin, Texas where she continued to work in the outpatient clinical setting. In 2013 Michelle opened up her own practice, MK Manual Therapy. She also joined the University of St. Augustine as a faculty lab instructor for Instructional Delivery for the DPT program. For the past 10 years she continues to teach in 7 different classes for musculoskeletal therapy, soft tissue massage and movement science exercises. She has enjoyed this immensely, as she gets to impart her knowledge while mentoring the next generation of Physical Therapists.
